Support Us

You want to know where your schmeckles are going

So you’re thinking about throwing a couple schmeckles our way. That’s cool, but I’d want to know too before I tossed my precious few schmeckles into someone else’s hat. Lets talk about money.

First, know that we really appreciate your support. Like, really appreciate it. Your support makes RallySec possible. We do this for you, and without you there’s not really a point.

Second, supporting us by any means allows us do this without having to sellout to corporate sponsors, change our format and content to be more corporate friendly, and potentially have to shill products that none of us really care about (we know its humans that make security work, not products).

Our goals for this are to make a freely available podcast that shares insights from our experiences in the industry, to help others learn, but also so we can learn from folks who participate in the conversation in Twitch chat or via Twitter. We don’t know everything :)

For Season 1 (the first 100 episodes that ran 2016-2018) our biggest cost was web hosting. Distributing 130MB-ish files in this decade doesn’t seem like a big deal.. and it wasn’t. The biggest issue was doing it quickly and cheaply. We had built our webstack using AWS, but we found as we grew to 3000+ regular listeners that we would regularly get charged $80+ a month for just data transfer. This doesn’t sound like much, but to stay true to our goals we needed to reduce this. This next season we’ve moved our webstack to a Jekyll site built on Github with LFS. They don’t sponsor us, but.. we appreciate them footing the bill for this :)

To us, RallySec means being active in rallying our community members, helping recover and return to fighting the good fight. Each day brings news of more breaches, vulnerabilities, and bad news. Its easy to succumb to burn out, to get depressed and despondent, to feel hopeless. We wanted a place that we could lift up spirits and have a good time, as well as share knowledge and information with the community.

Here are some ways you can support us directly:

  • Donate via PayPal (This is the lowest overhead option, most of the $ actually goes towards us)
  • Subscribe on Twitch(cheapest if you have Amazon Prime, you get 1 free subscription per month)
  • Support us via Patreon
  • Use our Amazon Associates links

We’re not sponsored by any of these companies we linked to, and we work hard to ensure we have little to no overhead so that we can keep doing RallySec as cheaply as possible. This allows us to speak freely about issues and topics that our sponsors might push back against.
