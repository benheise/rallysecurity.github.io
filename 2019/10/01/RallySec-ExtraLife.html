RallySec ExtraLife 2019-10-01 00:00:00 -0400

This past Tuesday, I announced that we are organizing a D&D 5E event supporting the ExtraLife charity. Rendition Infosec has chosen to sponsor this event, and the players are donating their time and help organizing and creating it. This is going to be fun!

![Banner](https://rallysecurity.com/img/extralife-event-banner.png width=50)

What it is

We’re going to be streaming the game live via Twitch Viewers and Donors can both help the kids and affect the game directly by donating to ExtraLife! All proceeds donated from Twitch subscriptions and bits are being given to the RallySec ExtraLife campaign at the end of the night.

When you donate, you get to pick from a list of priced actions or effects that the donor can have on the game as seen on the following chart:

When is it?

Oct 19th from 2:30pm EDT to 7:45pm EDT. Our loose schedule of events is below: 2:30pm - Start stream with sponsor scene 2:45pm - Introduction to the stream, what it is, and what its for. Thank everyone who helped make it a thing. 3:00pm - Thank Sponsors 3:05pm - Introduce players and their characters 3:10pm - Scenario 1 4:00pm - 10 minute break 4:10pm - Scenario 2 5:00pm - 30 minute mid-stream break with sponsor scene 5:30pm - Scenario 3 6:20pm - 10 minute break 6:30pm - D&D Battle Royale Free for all in the arena Twitchchat gets to play a monster 7:20pm - Tally total donations 7:30pm - Thank donors, thank Sponsors, player’s self promote their twitter/books/etc 7:45pm - End stream

How can we help?

If you can help financially, donating directly to the campaign is going to help the kids a lot.

Even if you can’t help monetarily, helping with retweets or shares will get the word out and bring awareness, which will help a lot!

-Ben

About the Author

Ben Heise (@benheise) is an information security professional who specializes in performing penetration testing, adversarial (red team) operations, and studying the history, tools, techniques, and procedures of “APTs”. He’s a US Army veteran looking to give back to the community, help others, and make the world a better place. His blog is over at https://benjaminheise.com
